About Me

Hello again, my name is Sandra and I’m in the 4th semester of studying communication design at Hof University. I’m 19 years old and originally from a small town near Landshut. In 2020 I finished my A levels and started to study right away. I’ve always loved to be creative and to help people out, therefore communication design was my first choice. And I’m still very happy with my decision. My main emphasis can be found in the sectors of editorial-design, graphic design, social design and photography. I’m also very interested in UX design, interaction design and building websites. I’m a very team-minded person, who especially loves to work with all types of print media. I set a high value on precision and communication.
