Skillset

The Adobe Creative Cloud forms a constant part of my projects. Therefore I have mastered the basic skills in every program. However, I am most familiar with InDesign, Lightroom Classic, PremierePro and Photoshop. Over the last two years I was also able to get to know a lot of different printing media such as silkscreen printing, riso printing or linocut. Since last semester I am very familiar with developing UX-Prototypes in Figma or Adobe XD. For websites I mostly use HTML and CSS or Webflow. I was also able to get to know basic skills in p5.js and JavaScript. I especially like to get to know new techniques.
