Current Projects

Glaubenssache

Our main focus during the 4th semester was to work with the city of Münchberg and the people living there. As a part of this project, I created a city map on the subject of the diversity of religion in Münchberg. I mainly worked with photography and illustration. To show the variety of beliefs, I added interviews with inhabitants of Münchberg. I especialliy wanted to include personal stories and experiences to make my project more relatable and understandable.

Planquadrat

Documentation and exhibition of a photography workshop in my 4th semester in cooperation with street photographer Margret Hoppe. During the 4th semester I got the opportunity to participate in a photography workshhop. The main goal was to show the beauty and diversity of the city of Münchberg. Every student was assigned a grid square of Münchberg randomly. We spent the whole day taking photos in these grid squares. Subsequently, all photography series were talked about and compiled.

Finished Projects

Das Reis(e)korn

Conception, illustration and production of the book “Das Reis(e)korn” During my 3rd Semester I concepted, illustrated and produced a book about a single rice grain and the journey it has to go on in order to be sold an consumed in Germany. The illustrations series, which comprises 15 pictures, were realized with linocut. My intention was to lead the reader through simple structures and a guiding concept. Since India is one of the most important exporters for rice, I decided to let the journey start there, more precisely in the state Tamil Nadu.

FAST-Test

Conception, Illustration and designing of the app “FAST-Test”, which is meant to help stroke patients and relatives. This project was a group project of a fellow student and me. In order to build a functional and working prototype, we spent a lot of time on the conception, which included personas, user stories and user journeys. We also talked to a neurologist about our app. The design is built very simple to guarantee a fast use without difficulty in case of emergency.

Von Aubergine bis Zucker

A dictionary about German words with Arabic roots. This group project was developed during the 2nd semester. Every student picked a word and created an illustration and a poetical text for this word. I picked the word “Safran”. Since we were not able to meet in person because of the pandemic, we chose the kitchen lithography for our illustrations.
