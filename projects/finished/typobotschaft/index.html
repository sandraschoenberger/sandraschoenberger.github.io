Typobotschaft

A critical message created during the first lockdown.

The first lockdown has been very difficult for many comapnies, especialle smaller family-buisnesses. To give these people a voice, we created individual letterings to show the problem and to critizise.

I went for the very unique writing that can be found on a hotel and restaurant in Landshut called “Goldene Sonne”. I studied the single letters and put them together forming a new sentence.

Later I presented it photographically. I decided to make it look like a protest sign and gave it a selfmade look.
