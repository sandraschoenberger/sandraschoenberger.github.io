Von Aubergine bis Zucker

A dictionary about German words with Arabic roots.

This group project was developed during the 2nd semester. Every student picked a word and created an illustration and a poetical text for this word. I picked the word “Safran”. Since we were not able to meet in person because of the pandemic, we chose the kitchen lithography for our illustrations.
