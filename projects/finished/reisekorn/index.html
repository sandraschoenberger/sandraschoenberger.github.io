Das Reis(e)korn

Conception, illustration and production of the book “Das Reis(e)korn”

During my 3rd Semester I concepted, illustrated and produced a book about a single rice grain and the journey it has to go on in order to be sold an consumed in Germany. The illustrations series, which comprises 15 pictures, were realized with linocut. My intention was to lead the reader through simple structures and a guiding concept.

Since India is one of the most important exporters for rice, I decided to let the journey start there, more precisely in the state Tamil Nadu. Along my researches I learned a lot about traditional harvesting techniques but also the grievance and poverty most people in India live in. In addition, the book shows delivery methods and distances.

This book is meant to educate and flag many things up. It was important for me to give the people who live and work in Tamil Nadu a voice.
