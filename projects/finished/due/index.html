FAST-Test

Conception, Illustration and designing of the app “FAST-Test”, which is meant to help stroke patients and relatives.

This project was a group project of a fellow student and me. In order to build a functional and working prototype, we spent a lot of time on the conception, which included personas, user stories and user journeys. We also talked to a neurologist about our app. The design is built very simple to guarantee a fast use without difficulty in case of emergency.

The prototype was realized in Figma and includes a mobile and smart watch version.
