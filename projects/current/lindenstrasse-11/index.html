Lindenstraße 11

Conception of different exhibitions in a small, vacant store in the center of Münchberg.

In April 2022 we started the project called “Lindenstraße 11”. We renovated the vacant store and began using it as a place to show our university projects. We wanted to invite people from Münchberg and explain our work to them. The first exhibition showed books we finished in our 3rd semester. The next one was more like a dialogue between us and Münchberg. People were able to visit the shop and write short ideas, suggestions and personal experiences about the city. After that, we concepted a night-exhibition with fluorescent tape and LEDs. Towards the end of the semester we planned a finissage for all projects that were finished during the semester.
