Mauergestaltung

Designing and painting of a mural for our campus.

In order to make our campus more welcoming and well-looking, we created a mural for the wall leading to the main entrance. We included symbols that resemble our campus and the study course communication design and worked with patterns as well. We started to place it with neon-tape. Unfortunately the tape detached during a heavy rain, even though we tested its weathering resistance.

However we used the remaining tape as a stencil and just painted over it. We chose bright colours as the mural functiones as an invitation to get to know the campus.
