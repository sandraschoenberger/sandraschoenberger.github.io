Planquadrat

Documentation and exhibition of a photography workshop in my 4th semester in cooperation with street photographer Margret Hoppe.

During the 4th semester I got the opportunity to participate in a photography workshhop. The main goal was to show the beauty and diversity of the city of Münchberg. Every student was assigned a grid square of Münchberg randomly. We spent the whole day taking photos in these grid squares. Subsequently, all photography series were talked about and compiled. Out of these photos, 9 postcards were produced.

A fellow student and me were responsible for the production of the postcards and the presentation of this project within the scope of our exhibition towards the end of the semester. We also created a flyer that comes with the postcards and shows the grid squares the photos were taken in.
