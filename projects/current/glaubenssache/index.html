Glaubenssache

A city map to show the diversity of religion in Münchberg.

Our main focus during the 4th semester was to work with the city of Münchberg and the people living there. As a part of this project, I created a city map on the subject of the diversity of religion in Münchberg.

I mainly worked with photography and illustration. To show the variety of beliefs, I added interviews with inhabitants of Münchberg. I especialliy wanted to include personal stories and experiences to make my project more relatable and understandable. I really enjoyed the social aspect of my project. Even though religion is a sensitive topic to talk about, I found a lot of stories that are definitely worth sharing.

I folded and tacked the city maps by myself. Alltogether, an edition of 100 maps has been produced.
